- Lede has a critical social mission to transport electricity to its customers
- Their goal is to ensure that customers have electricity available 99.98% of the time
- They have seen some impact from the new pricing model, but it is difficult to determine if it is due to the new net lease or high electricity prices
- Lede has had a high level of investments and expects it to continue due to planned large investments
- One challenge they face is accessing capacity from the central grid
- There are concerns about the time-consuming licensing processes before construction can begin.
