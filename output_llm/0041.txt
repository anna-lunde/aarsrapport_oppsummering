- High energy prices have affected the year, but there has been steady customer growth and demand for district heating.
- Customers have benefited from long contracts and lower district heating prices compared to electricity prices.
- Plans to build a large heat accumulator tank were replaced by the discovery of large unused underground storage rooms at Herøya.
- An agreement has been made with Yara and Herøya Industrial Park to explore the technical aspects and develop a business model for a symbiosis project.
- The company has made efforts to increase visibility through yellow cars and highlighting the concept of "surplus energy".
- The focus is on utilizing resources that would otherwise go to waste, such as using water for multiple purposes.
- The goal is to find more solutions to better utilize energy resources.
