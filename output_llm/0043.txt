- Skagerak Energipartner is an active owner and developer of companies in new growth areas.
- In 2021, they acquired Isola Solar and brought it under Skagerak Energipartner.
- From January 2022, all their partially owned companies in new growth areas are now under Skagerak Energipartner.
- The company's philosophy is to be a platform for growth outside of its current core business.
- They actively seek new areas for growth through acquiring stakes in companies or scaling up their own innovations.
- They are interested in companies that fit their strategy and have growth potential.
- They have invested in biogas, solar, and technological solutions that can contribute to their existing business or fit into innovation or development projects.
- They have a wide range of ownership stakes in different companies.
