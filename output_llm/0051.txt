- Skagerak Energi is focused on managing energy resources responsibly
- They aim to find solutions to energy challenges society is facing
- They have implemented a project called "Mobile Energy for emission-free construction sites" which involves moving energy to where it is needed using large batteries
- Pilot project was successfully conducted in 2022 and containers were used for various purposes such as powering construction equipment and providing extra electricity at a festival
- Skagerak Energi has also developed a platform called "Smart EV charging" which allows electric vehicle owners to optimize their charging based on demand and be financially rewarded
- They are working on a project called "Next Generation Virtual Power Plant" to aggregate flexible resources on the production and consumption side
- Skagerak Energi is committed to being part of the solution and focusing on new business and innovation in renewable energy.
