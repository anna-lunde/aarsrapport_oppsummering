- Skagerak Energi has developed mobile charging stations that can be placed in areas with high activity and demand for car charging
- The stations offer fast charging and can charge up to five electric cars simultaneously
- The pilot project has been successful at ski resorts and other locations, and has gained attention from various industries
- Skagerak Energi is also working on hydrogen as an energy carrier, but has decided to leave the Greenbulk project
- The company has established Skagerak Energitjenester, a subsidiary that aims to realize the potential of various projects and address challenges related to balance in the energy market.
