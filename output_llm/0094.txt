- Skagerak Energi undergoes a generational change and recruits 40-60 people annually.
- In 2022, 70 people were recruited, including apprentices and trainees.
- Employer branding activities are actively pursued to ensure Skagerak remains an attractive workplace.
- Efforts are made to ensure a good onboarding process for new employees, including a digital approach.
- Onboarding content is being improved with an introduction day for new employees and extended onboarding programs for both new and existing employees.
- Efforts are made to enhance offboarding processes, as an effective and respectful process can strengthen the company's reputation as an employer.
- An effective offboarding process is seen as a source of knowledge and values employees beyond their contributions.
