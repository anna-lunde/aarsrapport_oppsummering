- Skagerak Energi AS is a Norwegian company owned by Statkraft Industrial Holding AS, Skien Kommune, Porsgrunn Kommune, and Bamble Kommune.
- Skagerak Energi AS follows the International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS) for its financial reporting.
- The company uses simplified measurement and recognition rules for certain assets and depreciation methods.
- The financial statements include comparative figures for the previous year.
- The consolidated financial statements show the financial results and position of Skagerak Energi AS and its controlled subsidiaries.
- Transactions between the subsidiaries and eliminations of intercompany transactions are included in the consolidated financial statements.
- Acquisitions are recorded at the transaction date, and identifiable assets, liabilities, and contingent liabilities are recorded at their fair value.
- Investments in associated companies are treated using the equity method.
- Purchases of associated companies are recorded at cost.
- The company's share of the associated company's profit, adjusted for depreciation and differences in accounting principles, is shown separately in the consolidated financial statements.
